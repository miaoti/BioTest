Excerpted from PR #1554 (https://github.com/samtools/htsjdk/pull/1554)
and its test method `testGenotypeCountsRespectGenotypeFilters`.

> VariantContext.getCalledChrCount() iterates every genotype and counts
> its alleles. The method did not check the per-genotype FT filter
> field, so genotypes marked FT=<something non-PASS> contributed to
> AC/AN/AF even though the spec treats FT as a genotype-level exclude.
> Downstream tools that recompute AC/AN/AF from the genotype matrix
> therefore disagreed with htsjdk's in-place INFO values for any VCF
> with per-sample filters applied.

Fix commit merged 2021-06-10, shipped in htsjdk 3.0.0 (2022-06-06).
