Excerpted from PR #1592 (https://github.com/samtools/htsjdk/pull/1592).

> CRAM's SC (scores) data series is a delta-encoded quality stream.
> htsjdk's normalization pass misreads the delta direction when the
> per-record quality block is empty, producing QUAL arrays that are
> off by a constant offset from htslib's output. Discovered during
> hts-specs compliance testing.

Fix commit: 965ec81. Regression test: a CRAM with SC-encoded records
interleaved with empty-quality records added under
`test/resources/htsjdk/samtools/cram/` in the PR.
