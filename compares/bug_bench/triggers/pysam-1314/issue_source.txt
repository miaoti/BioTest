Excerpted from issue #1314
(https://github.com/pysam-developers/pysam/issues/1314).

> I'm editing VCF headers by hand, adding contigs in a new order, and
> copying records through. `rec.chrom` reports the right contig name
> on every record I fetch, but on the output VCF every record ends up
> on my first registered contig. Looks like `VariantFile.write` is
> re-mapping to header-contig-index-0 instead of matching by name.
>
> Reproduction:
>   hdr = pysam.VariantHeader()
>   hdr.contigs.add("mychr", length=1000)
>   hdr.contigs.add("Horvu_VADA_Un01G000200.1", length=2673)
>   src = pysam.VariantFile("input.vcf")   # records on Horvu_VADA_Un01G...
>   dst = pysam.VariantFile("out.vcf", "w", header=hdr)
>   for rec in src: dst.write(rec)
>   # out.vcf now shows CHROM=mychr for every record.

Fix landed in pysam 0.23.0 release notes as "VariantFile.write()
contig lookup by name, not index (#1314)".
